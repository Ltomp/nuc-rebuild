Three machines, three jobs

Split by workload rather than crammed onto one box: the Zigbee coordinator is network-attached (TCP, not USB), so Home Assistant's physical location is free to change. Frigate and the LLM both want GPU time, so they're deliberately on separate iGPUs (UHD 770 vs Arc) rather than competing on the NUC.
